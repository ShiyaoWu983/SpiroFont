How does it work?
Select a shape for your spirograph typeface pattern. Choose a color group for your pattern. Select a size for your shape to create your pattern. Decide whether you want to draw the pattern yourself or have our tool draw it for you. Enter the 8 letters that you want to include in your pattern. Preview the result and make any final adjustments before downloading. Enjoy creating your own unique SPIROFONT! - Shiyao
